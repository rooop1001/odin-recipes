Tres Leches

This delicious tres leches cake features three types of milk:

  • Whole Milk
  • Condensed Milk
  • Evaporated Milk

How to Make a Tres Leches Cake

  1. Make the cake
    • Sift the dry ingredients together in a bowl
    • Beat the butter and sugar together in another bowl.
    • Beat in the eggs and vanilla
    • Pour the batter into a prepared pan
    • Bake until a toothpick comes out clean
    • Pierce the cake all over
  2. Soak in Milk
    • Mix the three milks together in a bowl
    • Pour the mixture over the cooled sponge cake
  3. Top with whipped cream
    • Beat the whipping cream, sugar, and vanilla until stiff peaks form
    • Spread over the cake
